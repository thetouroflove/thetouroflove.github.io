On August 19, 2019, Surya asked Alex to marry him. She said yes!

It was a warm Monday afternoon on the shores of Lake Michigan. Lauren (Alex's little sister) was in town visiting that weekend, but Surya had to go to work. Lauren convinced Alex to go for a manicure in Chicago, then take a long walk along the coast of the lake. Surya took the day off to go surprise Alex on the shoreline beach. She had no clue!

(Ok she actually figured it out almost immediately because Surya was being really obvious the night before. It still worked out!)
