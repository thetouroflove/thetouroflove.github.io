VIRTUAL WEDDING CEREMONY

OCTOBER 17th, 2020

Due to COVID-19, we have had to cancel our 2020 wedding celebration plans. We don't know how much longer the world will be like this, but we still wanted share the beginning of our union with all of our loved ones. We hope our slightly unconventional Zoom wedding will be a fun and happy way for all of us to come together and celebrate our marriage!

Below, you will find some recipes for our favorite meals, desserts, and drinks. In lieu of breaking bread together, we thought it would be fun to share some of these, in case you wanted to celebrate by making these on the day of the virtual wedding!

We love you, and look forward to celebrating with you all and seeing you in person again!

Love,
Alex and Surya

OUR FAVORITE HOME RECIPES

SURYA'S LEMON PASTA

This recipe was modified from one given to us by Alex's grandma! Since it's so delicious and quick to put together, it's quickly become one of our weekly staples.

INGREDIENTS
  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • ⅓ cup heavy cream
  • ⅓ cup olive oil
  • ⅓ cup (30 grams) of grated parmigiano reggiano
  • ¼ cup fresh lemon juice, plus zest of 1-2 lemons
  • Handful of chopped/chiffonaded basil
STEPS
  1. Bring pot of aggressively-salted water to a boil and begin to cook the pasta
  2. As the pasta is cooking, combine the heavy cream, olive oil, cheese, and lemon zest/juice in a heat-proof mixing bowl. Thoroughly whisk together until a cohesive sauce forms
  3. When the pasta is done, use tongs to directly move the spagetti from the pot to the mixing bowl - this will carry some pasta water with it that will help it come together. Toss and coat.
  4. Serve and garnish with the basil - don't skip this step! It really brings everything together.
