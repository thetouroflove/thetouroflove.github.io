ATLANTA

MEHENDI is the decoration of one's body with henna.
AASHIRWAD comes from the sanskrit word for blessing.

Both the Mehendi and Aashirwad ceremonies are
meaningful and symbolic Indian pre-wedding traditions.

In December of 2019, we traveled with Alex's family to Surya's childhood home in Atlanta. Surya and his parents moved to the US when he was only 4 years old. Being first generation immigrants, they didn't have a lot of relatives nearby. However, in Atlanta, Surya's family found the most amazing community of friends and neighbors. They became Surya's family, and we were so lucky to celebrate with them.

During the Mehendi ceremony, Alex and all the other women received beautiful henna decorations on their hands and arms. We also celebrated Surya's birthday!

The highlight of the Aashirwad ceremony is the blessing of the bride and groom. Each family came up to us and gave us their blessing for a long and happy marriage.
